> #Information

Variables = Points on a plane (x1, y1), (x2, y2)
Slope Formula: m = (y2-y1) / (x2-x1)

TO FIND Y-INTERCEPT FORM:
y - y1 = m(x - x1)

TO FIND STANDARD FORM:
(start with y-intercept answer)
Ax + By = C
RULES:
A = x coefficient
B = y coefficient
C = y-intercept
x MUST BE POSITIVE (mul/div everything by -1 if it is not),
NO FRACTIONS (mul everything by the denominator of the fractions you have)
