> #Information

Advertising here is allowed, but follow the guidelines given. Advertising servers can be done in #invites. Send your permanent invite link to us in DMs with a server description (optional, can be short and simple) and we will add it. Advertising for YouTube and other video services can be done in #videos. Do not advertise in #main-chat.

RULES FOR PUNISHMENT (these numbers may vary depending on what rule you broke.)


Non-admins:
First mute: usually 1-7 days, after 5 total warnings. Mutes may become hard mutes if you try to contradict the staff.
Second mute: usually 7-14 days, after 10 total warnings
Temp-ban: depending on what rule you have broken, these may vary greatly. The shortest may last for 14 days, while some last for 2 months. It really depends on what you did, and this punishment is given after 15 total warnings.
Perma-ban: after 25 total warnings, this punishment will be served, banning you permanetly from the server.

Admins/Mods:
Strike: after 3 warnings, admins will recive a strike.
Demotion: after 5 strikes for normal admins/mods, 10 strikes for head admins, or a supermajority vote (66%+) for owners, the person being punished will be demoted.
